Own your own data

Many systems use your data to profit by breaking down your associations and using this data to promote things to you. GangGo doesn't use your information for any reason other than enabling you to associate and share with others.

Choose your audience

GangGo's aspects enable you to impart to only those individuals you need to. You can be as open or as private as you prefer. Offer a clever photograph with the entire world, or a profound mystery just with your dearest companions. You're in charge.

Be who you want to be

A ton of systems demand that you use your genuine personality. Not GangGo. Here you can pick who you need to be, and share to such an extent or as meager about yourself as you need. It truly is dependent upon you how you need to communicate with other individuals.

Decentralization

GangGo is completely decentralized, and any person from any server can communicate among themselves! You can choose between registering at any of these other servers or you can set up your own server if you'd like.
