Welcome to GangGo!

GangGo is a decentralized social network written in GoLang. It uses the same federation library like Diaspora and therefore can participate in conversations like any other Pod!

Learn more about Diaspora »

Own your own data

Many networks use your data to make money by analysing your interactions and using this information to advertise things to you. GangGo doesn’t use your data for any purpose other than allowing you to connect and share with others.

Choose your audience

GangGo’s aspects allow you to share with just those people you want to. You can be as public or as private as you like. Share a funny photo with the whole world, or a deep secret just with your closest friends. You’re in control.

Be who you want to be

A lot of networks insist that you use your real identity. Not GangGo. Here you can choose who you want to be, and share as much or as little about yourself as you want. It really is up to you how you want to interact with other people.

Decentralization

GangGo is completely decentralized, and any person from any pod can communicate with one another! You can register at any of these other pods as well. You can also choose to set up your own pod if you'd like.

Make a contribution!

Make GangGo even better! Fork the project on GitHub make some changes, and submit a pull request.
